Selling Guidelines

Please follow these rules when listing your farm products:

  • All products must be accurately described with correct measurements
  • Organic products must be certified to be labeled as such
  • Provide clear photos of your actual products (not stock images)
  • Prices must be competitive with local market rates
  • Be honest about product availability and delivery timelines
  • Follow all local agricultural regulations and safety standards
  • Clearly state any special storage requirements
  • Update availability promptly when items are sold out
