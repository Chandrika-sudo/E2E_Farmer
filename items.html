Pricing Strategy

Research local market prices and consider your costs when setting prices. Competitive pricing attracts more buyers while ensuring your profitability.

  • Check competitor prices weekly
  • Adjust for quality differences
  • Offer bulk discounts strategically

Product Presentation

High-quality photos and detailed descriptions can increase sales by up to 30%. Show your products in natural light with minimal background distractions.

  • Use natural daylight for photos
  • Show products from multiple angles
  • Include a common object for scale

Delivery Options

Offer multiple delivery options when possible. Clearly communicate pickup locations and delivery timelines to avoid misunderstandings.

  • Provide local pickup options
  • Partner with reliable delivery services
  • Communicate clearly about timelines
